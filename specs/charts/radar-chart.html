Le composant <radar-chart> permet de comparer plusieurs variables simultanement.

Radar simple

<radar-chart
  x='[["RGAA", "DSFR", "Performance", "Securite", "Accessibilite"]]'
  y="[[85, 90, 75, 95, 88]]"
  selected-palette="default">
</radar-chart>

Comparaison multi-series

<radar-chart
  x='[["RGAA", "DSFR", "Perf", "Secu", "A11y"], ["RGAA", "DSFR", "Perf", "Secu", "A11y"]]'
  y="[[85, 90, 75, 95, 88], [70, 80, 90, 75, 82]]"
  selected-palette="categorical">
</radar-chart>
